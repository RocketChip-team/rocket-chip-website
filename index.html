What is Rocket-Chip ? :

We are a video-game making team of two devs. We love making video games and we are working since one year on pygame (sdl for python). We decided to begin a big project : a simple open source video-game engine (working with pygame) for making video game programming easier for python users. We will post all changes on this site and on our github account. We hope we make good code. If it's not, please feedback and inform us !
