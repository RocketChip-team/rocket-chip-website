What is Rocket-Chip ? :

We are a video-game making team of two programmers. We love make video games and we are working since one year on pygame (sdl for python). We decided to begin a big project : a simple open source video-game engine (working with pygame) for make video game programming easier for beginners. We will post all changes on this site and on our github account. We hope we do good code. If it's not, please feedback and inform us !
